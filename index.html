About me

Hi everyone, I am Christina Bornberg and this is my project collection website! I have an electronics/computer science/project management background, which I like to apply to both the medical and space field.

For computer-aided diagnosis, I enjoy working with deep learning. My research interest covers ordinal classification, partial labels, semi-supervised learning, semi-automated training-data annotation, multi-task learning and curriculum learning. I'm currently a scholar of the Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree "MSc in Medical Imaging and Applications" and am based in Italy.

I also enjoy space mission design which I spend my Friday nights with. Together with my study colleagues from Loughborough I am a co-lead of the Conex Research team and do project management, electronics and planetary science for our space missions.
