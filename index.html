About me

Hi everyone, I am Christina Bornberg and this is my project collection website! I have an electronics/computer science/project management background, which I like to apply to both the medical and space field.

Medical: For computer-aided diagnosis, I enjoy working with deep learning, with my favourite application being ophthalmology. My main research interest is the interpretability of modular convnets for retinal disease understanding. Previously, I completed the Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree "MSc in Medical Imaging and Applications" and was a researcher at the Singapore Eye Research Institute. The next goal is to finish the masters degree "Vision Science and Investigative Ophthalmology" at the University of Miami.

Space and Climate: After moving on from Conex Research after 3 amazing years focusing on space mission design and the solar system, I am now part of the senv team. We are working on satellite image analysis, focusing on the la Nina and el Nino phenomenon! My main interest here are bees and cherry blossom!

Currently based in: Miami
