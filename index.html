About me

Hi everyone, I am Christina Bornberg and this is my project collection website! I have an electronics/computer science/project management background, which I like to apply to both the medical and space field.

For computer-aided diagnosis, I enjoy working with deep learning, with my favourite application being ophthalmology. My research interest covers especially disentangled learning, modularity in convnets and semi-supervised learning. I just completed the Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree "MSc in Medical Imaging and Applications" and am continuing working with the Singapore Eye Research Institute where I also did my Master's thesis.

Also my space research is making progress! After moving on from Conex Research after 3 amazing years, I started a solar flare project and will work on climate research for Earth, focusing on the la Nina and el Nino phenomenon!

Currently based in: Singapore
