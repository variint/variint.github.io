About me

Hi everyone, I am Christina Bornberg and this is my project collection website! I have an electronics/computer science/project management background, which I like to apply to both the medical and space field.

For computer-aided diagnosis, I enjoy working with deep learning, with my favourite application being ophthalmology. My research interest covers especially ordinal classification, partial labels and semi-supervised learning. I'm currently a scholar of the Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree "MSc in Medical Imaging and Applications" and am doing my Master's thesis at the Singapore Eye Research Institute.

I also enjoy space mission design which I spend my Friday nights with. Together with my former study colleagues from Loughborough University (UK) I am a co-lead of the Conex Research team and do project management, digital systems (electronics, data science) and planetary science for our space missions.
